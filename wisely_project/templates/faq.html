    Can I change my pledge?

    Unfortunately, once you make a pledge for an entire course, you cannot change your pledge for that course. This is to help keep you motivated and committed.

    Can I remove my pledge?

    Of course, we understand sometimes circumstances can come up. If that’s the case, please fill out a Request for Leave of Compassionate Reason. Once approved, your pledge amount is safe and will stay in your account and you can come back at anytime to pledge it towards another course.

    What if I miss an assignment?

    We get it. Sometime life happens or an assignment sucks and for whatever reason, you just didn't complete it on time. That’s why everyone gets two ‘Freebies’ per course that you can apply to any assignment or quiz throughout the course. Use them sparingly!

    How much will I be rewarded?

    Rewards will be calculated when the course ends, determined by the number of users that have completed their goals. Rewards are divided evenly amongst all of the users, regardless of how much money you pledge.

    What counts as ‘completion’?

    Completion of a course means you have handed in all quizzes or assignment. You must submit the assignment by the deadline of your course and you must obtain a minimum grade of 60% for it to count.
