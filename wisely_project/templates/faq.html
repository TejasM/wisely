    Questions? We have answers.

    You can find some answers to commonly asked questions below.

    • All topics
    • Payments
    • Technical

    How much should I pledge?

    That’s up to you! Wisely encourages you to pledge the amount that you think will motivate you to achieve your goals, with a minimum of $10. Your reward will be calculated based on what grade you aim to achieve in the course, not how much you pledge.

    What should my goal be?

    This one’s up to you! Take a minute to review the course topics, assignments and expectations and consider factors such as:

    • How much time will I have to commit?
    • How important is the course material to me?

    Push yourself! Remember, the higher your goal, the more you’ll be rewarded when you reach your goal.

    Can I change my pledge?

    Unfortunately, once you make a pledge for an entire course, you cannot change your pledge for that course. This is to help keep you motivated and committed.

    Can I remove my pledge?

    Of course, we understand sometimes circumstances can come up. If that’s the case, please fill out a Request for Leave of Compassionate Reason. Once approved, your pledge amount is safe and will stay in your account and you can come back at anytime to pledge it towards another course.

    How much will I be rewarded?

    Pledges from all of the users that do not reach their goal are pooled. The pool is divided amongst all of the users that were successful. The rewards are allocated based on the goal you set for yourself. If you pledge to obtain 90% in the course, you will be rewarded more than if you pledged to 60%.

    What counts as ‘achieving my goal’?

    When you make a pledge, you will be asked to set a goal for performance in the course. If your final mark in the course is equal to or above the goal you set for yourself, congratulations! You have successfully achieved your goal.

    How are payments accepted?

    Wisely currently accepts payment through Visa, MasterCard and all other credit cards.

    Will I get charged right away?

    No, we will only charge your pledge amount at the end of your course if you do not complete your goal. You will not be charged any money if you complete your course. We collect your credit card information upfront to help keep you motivated.

    When can I collect my reward?

    Once your rewards account reaches $10, you can cash in your reward through paypal. Well done on your hard work! Alternatively, you can continue to let it accumulate and cash in on various gift cards and prizes from our sponsors. Lastly, once you accumulate rewards, you have the option to pledge using your reward account instead of cash.
    How does Wisely track my progress?

    Once you sign into your Coursera account, we will be able to help you track your progress through information we gather from Coursera. Don’t worry, all of your information is secured and will never be shared with anyone.
