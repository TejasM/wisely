What is Wisely?

Wisely incentivizes you to meet your personal learning goals on Coursera. Stay on track and get rewarded for your hard work.

Connect with your Coursera account - we will get all of your active courses for you.

Make a pledge for each course. Ask yourself, how much will it take for you to stay on track? Don’t worry, we won’t share your bank information with anyone.

Get on it! Time for you to put in the work. Be sure to complete all assignments & quizzes by the course deadlines for it to count. Keep track of your progress with our progress tracker and let us know if an assignment or quiz was not verified.

Follow your classmates. See how you stack up against your friends and online classmates by following their profile. Invite others to follow you on Wisely to keep you motivated.

Once the course ends, we collect all of the pledges of students who did not reach their goal, and split it amongst all those who completed their goal.

Collecting your rewards - Once your reward account is $10 (above your pledge amount), you can cash out through paypal. Enjoy your hard-earned cash!
