What is Wisely?

Wisely incentivizes you to meet your personal learning goals on Coursera. Stay on track and get rewarded for your hard work.

Connect

Sign In with your Coursera account - we will get all of your active courses for you.

Commit.

Make a pledge for each course. Ask yourself, how much will it take for you to stay on track?

Get on it!

Time for you to put in the work. Be sure to complete all assignments & quizzes by the course deadlines for it to count.

Follow.

See how you stack up against your friends and online classmates. Invite others to follow you to keep you motivated.

Reward

Once the course ends, we collect all of the pledges of students who did not reach their goal, and split it amongst all those who completed their goal.

Collect

Once your reward account is over $10, you can cash out through paypal. Enjoy your hard-earned cash!
