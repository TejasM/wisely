How do you get rewarded?





Wisely will only collects your pledge upfront to help you stay committed to your learning.



Once you complete your course, you will receive full refund of your pledge along with a reward for your hard work.



Rewards are paid from money collected from users in your course who do not reach their goal. The higher you set your goal, the higher your reward.



Your pledge amount is for your personal motivation and does not impact your reward amount.
