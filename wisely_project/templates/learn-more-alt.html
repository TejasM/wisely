What is Wisely?

Wisely helps you to meet your personal learning goals on Coursera and edX. Stay on track and get rewards for your hard work.

Connect.

Sign In with your Coursera or edX account - we will connect all of your active courses for you.

Commit.

Make a pledge for each course. Ask yourself, how much will it take for you to stay on track?

Get on it!

Follow.

See how you stack up against your friends and online classmates. Invite others to follow you to keep you motivated.

Reward.

Once the course ends, we collect all of the pledges of students who did not reach their goal, and divide it amongst all those who completed their goal.

Collect.

Once your reward account is over $10, you can cash out through paypal. Enjoy your hard-earned cash!
